About This Learnership

Telkom's learnership trains learners in IT, network administration, and cybersecurity, fields in high and rising demand. Accredited through the MICT SETA, it blends theory with hands-on telecoms experience.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 with strong Maths and Science
  • Interest in networks, IT, or cybersecurity
  • Unemployed youth (roughly 18–35)
  • Basic computer literacy

Stipend & What You Get

Telkom's learnership pays a stipend of around R5 500 a month over 12 months, plus an accredited IT/networking qualification. Cybersecurity skills in particular are scarce and well rewarded.

Application Process

5

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • Network Support Technician
  • IT Support / Help Desk Analyst
  • Junior Cybersecurity Analyst (with further study)
  • Systems Administrator
  • A pathway toward cloud or security specialisations
