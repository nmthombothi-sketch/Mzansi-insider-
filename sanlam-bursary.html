About This Bursary

The Sanlam Bursary supports students in actuarial science, finance, IT, and related fields central to the financial-services sector. It covers the full cost of study and offers vacation work and graduate opportunities within the Sanlam group.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Strong matric or current university results, especially Mathematics
  • Studying actuarial science, finance, IT or a related degree
  • Academic merit
  • Interest in financial services

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration
  • Accommodation
  • Books and a living allowance
  • Vacation work and mentorship
  • Potential employment with Sanlam
