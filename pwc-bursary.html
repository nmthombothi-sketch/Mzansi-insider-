About This Bursary

The PwC Bursary supports students studying accounting, auditing, and finance, typically on the path to qualifying as a CA(SA). It covers the cost of study and offers a SAICA training contract (articles) at PwC, one of the Big Four firms.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Accepted for BCom Accounting at a SAICA-accredited university
  • Strong matric results, especially Mathematics
  • Commitment to the CA(SA) pathway
  • Demonstrated academic merit

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration
  • Accommodation and books
  • A living allowance
  • Mentorship and vacation work
  • A SAICA training contract (articles) at PwC

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the PwC bursary cover?
Full tuition, accommodation, books, and a living allowance, plus a SAICA training contract at PwC.
Does the PwC bursary lead to articles?
Yes. It typically includes a SAICA training contract (articles) at PwC, a key step toward qualifying as a CA(SA).
Who qualifies for the PwC bursary?
South African citizens studying BCom Accounting at a SAICA-accredited university, with strong Mathematics results and a commitment to the CA(SA) path.
When does the PwC bursary close?
Applications generally close in the second half of the year. Confirm the exact date on the official PwC site.
