About This Bursary

The Old Mutual Education Trust Bursary supports students in commerce, actuarial science, finance, and related fields. It covers the full cost of study and offers development support and vacation work, providing a pathway into the financial-services sector and the Old Mutual group.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Strong matric or current university results, especially in Mathematics
  • Studying or intending to study commerce, actuarial science, finance or a related degree
  • Academic merit and, in some cases, financial need
  • Interest in the financial-services sector

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration fees
  • Accommodation
  • Books and a living allowance
  • Vacation work and mentorship
  • Potential employment with Old Mutual

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Old Mutual bursary cover?
It typically covers full tuition, accommodation, books, and a living allowance, plus vacation work and mentorship within Old Mutual.
Which fields does the Old Mutual bursary fund?
Commerce, actuarial science, finance, and related fields aligned to the financial-services sector.
Who can apply for the Old Mutual bursary?
South African citizens with strong academic results, especially in Mathematics, studying or intending to study a relevant commerce or actuarial degree.
When does the Old Mutual bursary close?
Applications generally close in the second half of the year. Confirm the exact date on the official Old Mutual careers site.
