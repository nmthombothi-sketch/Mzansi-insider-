Source of Wealth

Caster Semenya's fortune of around $18 million in 2026 reflects years of prize money, bonuses, endorsements, and appearances built on one of the most dominant middle-distance records in athletics.

At her peak she was almost unbeatable over 800m — winning two Olympic golds and three world titles, with a 30-race winning streak over the distance — which made her highly attractive to sponsors.

Her profile has been shaped as much by her long legal battle over World Athletics' testosterone regulations; in July 2025 she won an appeal on the grounds that she had been denied a fair trial.
