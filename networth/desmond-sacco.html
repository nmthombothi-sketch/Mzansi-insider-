Source of Wealth

Desmond Sacco's fortune of roughly $2.5 billion in 2026 comes from the Assore Group, the mining company founded by his father that he built into a major producer of manganese, iron ore, and chrome.

His wealth is closely tied to Assore's stake in Assmang, a substantial producer of the ferrous metals essential to steel — commodities whose prices have benefited from sustained global demand.

As a private mining fortune, the precise figure is harder to pin down than for listed-share billionaires, but Sacco is consistently ranked among South Africa's wealthiest.
