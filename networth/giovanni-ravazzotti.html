Source of Wealth

Giovanni Ravazzotti's fortune, estimated at around $330 million, is built on Italtile, the tile and homeware retailer he founded after emigrating from Italy to Johannesburg in 1969, and on Ceramic Industries, a major tile and sanitaryware manufacturer.

By controlling both retail and manufacturing, the family captured value across the supply chain, and the businesses remain JSE-listed with the family holding significant stakes.

He has also chaired the Italian-South African Chamber of Trade and Industry, bridging the two business communities.

Lessons Learned

What aspiring builders can take away
  • Own the whole chain. Controlling retail and manufacturing captured more value
  • Back a growing market. Home improvement and tiling rode decades of construction demand
  • Start where you are. An immigrant with little built a listed empire from scratch
  • Keep it in the family. Careful succession preserved the business
