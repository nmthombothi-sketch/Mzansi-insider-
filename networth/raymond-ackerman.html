Source of Wealth

The Ackerman family fortune, estimated at around $300 million, is built almost entirely on Pick n Pay, the supermarket group with hundreds of stores across South Africa and neighbouring countries.

Raymond Ackerman, who died in 2023, founded the modern Pick n Pay after being fired from the Greatermans/Checkers group — buying four small stores and applying his philosophy of 'consumer sovereignty'.

The family retains a significant controlling stake in the JSE-listed group, and Raymond's widow Wendy and the wider family remain central to its ownership.
