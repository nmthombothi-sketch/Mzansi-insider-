Source of Wealth

Laurie Dippenaar's fortune of roughly $1.2 billion in 2026 comes from co-founding the businesses that became FirstRand — one of South Africa's biggest financial-services groups, spanning FNB, RMB, and WesBank.

His wealth is rooted in long-held equity stakes through vehicles such as RMB Holdings and RMI, built up over a career that helped shape modern South African banking and insurance.

He is regarded as one of the architects of a distinctly entrepreneurial banking culture, having started from a small merchant-banking base in the late 1970s.

Companies & Major Holdings
