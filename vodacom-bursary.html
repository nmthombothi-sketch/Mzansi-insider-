About This Bursary

The Vodacom External Bursary funds full-time students in STEM fields who may lack the resources to study. Beyond full financial cover, it provides a laptop and cellphone, an orientation workshop, structured vacation work, and work-readiness training for final-year students.

Requirements

  • South African citizen by birth
  • Full-time undergraduate study at an accredited SA institution
  • Grade 12 exemption with 70%+ in all subjects
  • Minimum 65% average if already at tertiary level
  • Studying a STEM field

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and accommodation
  • Meals and a book allowance
  • A laptop and cellphone
  • Structured vacation work programme
  • Wellness programme access and work-readiness training

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Vodacom bursary cover?
Full tuition, accommodation, meals, books, a laptop and cellphone, plus vacation work and wellness support.
Who qualifies for the Vodacom bursary?
South African citizens by birth studying STEM fields, with Grade 12 exemption and 70%+ in all subjects, or a 65% average at tertiary level.
Does the Vodacom bursary include a laptop?
Yes. Recipients receive a laptop and cellphone, along with structured vacation work and work-readiness training.
When does the Vodacom bursary close?
Applications typically open mid-year. Confirm the exact closing date on the official Vodacom site.
