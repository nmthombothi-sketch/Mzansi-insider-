About This Learnership

Impala Platinum's learnership trains learners in fitting and engineering disciplines for the mining sector, combining structured theory with hands-on work at its operations. It is a recognised route toward becoming a qualified artisan.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 with Mathematics and Physical Science
  • Interest in fitting, mechanical or engineering work
  • Unemployed youth (roughly 18–35)
  • Physically fit for mining environments
  • Host-community candidates often prioritised

Stipend & What You Get

Impala's learnership pays a stipend of around R6 000 a month, plus an accredited technical qualification and a path toward an artisan trade test, which unlocks strong mining-sector pay.

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • Fitter or Mechanical Assistant
  • Qualified Artisan (with trade test)
  • Plant or Engineering Technician
  • Maintenance specialist (with experience)
  • Further study toward engineering
