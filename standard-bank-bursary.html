About This Bursary

The Standard Bank Group Bursary supports students in accounting, finance, information technology and related commerce fields that feed the bank's talent pipeline. Alongside full financial support, recipients receive mentorship and vacation work, and strong performers often progress into graduate programmes and employment at the group.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Strong academic results in Mathematics and relevant subjects
  • Studying an eligible commerce, finance or IT degree
  • Financial need considered alongside merit

What It Covers

  • Full tuition fees
  • Accommodation
  • Laptop and learning materials
  • Mentorship and vacation work
  • Potential employment at Standard Bank
