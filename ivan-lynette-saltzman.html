Source of Wealth

Ivan and Lynette Saltzman's combined fortune of roughly $1 billion in 2026 comes from Dis-Chem Pharmacies, the retail pharmacy and health chain they founded and built over four decades.

Dis-Chem listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange in 2016, crystallising the family's wealth, and the couple retain a significant shareholding in the business they started from a single store.

The chain's model — combining pharmacy with beauty, healthy foods, and supplements at scale — turned a modest start-up into a household name with hundreds of outlets.
