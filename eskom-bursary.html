About This Bursary

The Eskom Engineering Bursary Programme supports students in electrical, mechanical, civil and related engineering disciplines critical to the energy sector. It combines full financial support with vacation work at Eskom, giving recipients practical experience alongside their studies and a potential route into the utility after graduating.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Strong Mathematics and Physical Science results
  • Studying or intending to study an eligible engineering degree
  • Academic merit and, in some cases, financial need

What It Covers

  • Full tuition fees
  • Accommodation
  • Monthly living allowance
  • Prescribed textbooks
  • Vacation work at Eskom

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Eskom bursary cover?
The Eskom Engineering Bursary typically covers full tuition, accommodation, a monthly allowance and textbooks, along with vacation work at Eskom.
Which fields does the Eskom bursary support?
Electrical, mechanical, civil and related engineering disciplines that are critical to the power and energy sector.
Who can apply for the Eskom bursary?
South African citizens with strong Mathematics and Physical Science results who are studying, or about to study, an eligible engineering degree.
When does the Eskom bursary close?
Applications for 2026 close on 31 July 2026. Always confirm on the official Eskom careers site, as timelines shift each year.
