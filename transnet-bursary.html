About This Bursary

The Transnet Bursary Programme funds students in engineering disciplines and logistics-related fields that support South Africa's freight, rail, port, and pipeline infrastructure. It covers the full cost of study with an allowance and offers vacation work and employment prospects within the Transnet group.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Strong Mathematics and Physical Science results
  • Studying or intending to study engineering, logistics or a related field
  • Academic merit and, in some cases, financial need
  • Interest in the transport and logistics sector

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration fees
  • Accommodation
  • Monthly allowance and books
  • Vacation work within Transnet
  • Potential employment after graduating

What fields does the Transnet bursary fund?

Does the Transnet bursary lead to a job?

Who can apply for the Transnet bursary?
South African citizens with strong Mathematics and Physical Science results studying, or intending to study, engineering, logistics, or a related field.
