About This Learnership

Glencore's learnership provides technical and engineering workplace training in the mining sector, combining theory with practical experience at its operations. Mining offers some of the strongest technical pay premiums in the country.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 with Mathematics and Physical Science
  • Interest in mining and engineering
  • Unemployed youth (roughly 18–35)
  • Physically fit for mining environments
  • Often prioritised for host-community candidates

Stipend & What You Get

Glencore's learnership pays a stipend of around R6 000 a month, plus an accredited technical qualification. Mining's strong pay premium makes these technical skills especially valuable.

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • Mining or Engineering Assistant
  • Artisan (fitter, electrician, boilermaker) with trade test
  • Process or Plant Operator
  • Technical specialist (with experience)
  • Further study toward mining engineering
