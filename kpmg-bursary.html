About This Bursary

The KPMG Bursary funds accounting students toward the CA(SA) qualification, covering study costs and offering a SAICA training contract at KPMG. It is one of the well-known Big Four bursary routes into chartered accountancy.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Accepted for BCom Accounting at a SAICA-accredited university
  • Strong matric results, especially Mathematics
  • Commitment to qualifying as a CA(SA)
  • Academic merit and, in some cases, financial need

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration
  • Accommodation and books
  • A living allowance
  • Mentorship and vacation work
  • A SAICA training contract (articles) at KPMG

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the KPMG bursary worth?
Reports put the KPMG bursary at around R150 000, covering tuition, accommodation, books, and a living allowance, plus a training contract.
Does the KPMG bursary include articles?
Yes. It typically includes a SAICA training contract (articles) at KPMG toward the CA(SA) qualification.
Who qualifies for the KPMG bursary?
South African citizens studying BCom Accounting at a SAICA-accredited university with strong Mathematics and a commitment to the CA(SA) path.
When does the KPMG bursary close?
Applications generally close in the second half of the year. Confirm the exact date on the official KPMG site.
