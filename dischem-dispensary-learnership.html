About This Learnership

The Dis-Chem learnership trains learners in dispensary support and customer service within its pharmacies, leading to a recognised NQF Level 3 qualification and a practical route into pharmacy retail.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 with Mathematics and a science subject
  • Interest in pharmacy and customer service
  • Unemployed youth (roughly 18–34)
  • Reliable and customer-focused

Stipend & What You Get

The Dis-Chem learnership pays a stipend of around R4 500 a month over 12 months, plus an accredited pharmacist-assistant qualification, with a route into pharmacy-retail roles.

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • Qualified Pharmacist Assistant
  • Dispensary Support team member
  • Senior Pharmacist Assistant (with further study)
  • Retail healthcare team member
  • A stepping stone toward further pharmacy study
