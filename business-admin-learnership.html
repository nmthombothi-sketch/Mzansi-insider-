About This Learnership

The Business Administration learnership, coordinated through the Services SETA, prepares candidates for office and administrative careers. Training covers office management, document control, communication, and HR support, leading to an NQF Level 4 qualification.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 / Matric
  • Good communication and computer literacy
  • Unemployed youth (roughly 18–35)
  • Organised and detail-oriented

Stipend & What You Get

The business administration learnership pays a stipend of around R4 500 a month over 12 months, plus an NQF 4 qualification that is portable across almost every industry, since every organisation needs administrators.

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • Office Administrator / Administrative Assistant
  • Data Capturer or Document Controller
  • Receptionist or Office Coordinator
  • HR or Operations Assistant
  • A foundation for further business study

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the business administration learnership pay?
It pays a stipend of around R4 500 a month over 12 months, plus a Services SETA NQF 4 qualification.
Who can apply for a business administration learnership?
Unemployed South African youth with a matric certificate, good communication, and basic computer literacy.
Is business administration a good learnership?
Yes. The qualification is portable across virtually every industry, since all organisations need administrators.
Where do you find business administration learnerships?
They are advertised by companies funded by the Services SETA, on company careers pages and SETA portals throughout the year.
