About This Bursary

The Discovery Actuarial Bursary funds high-performing students pursuing a BSc in Actuarial Science. Given the long road to qualifying as an actuary, it pairs full financial cover with structured support toward the Actuarial Society exams, plus vacation work that builds real industry experience at Discovery.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Accepted for BSc Actuarial Science at a recognised university
  • Exceptional matric results, especially in Mathematics
  • Strong analytical ability and commitment to the actuarial path
  • Maintaining the high academic standard the qualification demands

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration fees
  • Accommodation and book allowances
  • Support toward Actuarial Society (ASSA) exams
  • Vacation work and mentorship at Discovery
  • A potential graduate role after qualifying

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Discovery Actuarial Bursary cover?
It typically covers full tuition, registration, accommodation, and book allowances, plus support toward the Actuarial Society exams and vacation work at Discovery.
What results do I need for an actuarial bursary?
Exceptional matric results are required, especially in Mathematics, because actuarial science is one of the most mathematically demanding degrees in the country.
Does the bursary help with actuarial exams?
Yes. A key feature is structured support toward the Actuarial Society of South Africa exams, central to qualifying as a Fellow (FASSA).
When does the Discovery Actuarial Bursary close?
Applications typically close mid-year. Confirm the exact date on the official Discovery careers site.
