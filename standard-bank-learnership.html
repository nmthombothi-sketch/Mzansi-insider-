About This Learnership

Standard Bank's learnership develops talent across retail and corporate banking, leading to an NQF 5 qualification. With mentorship and a structured graduate pipeline, it is a strong entry point into the financial-services sector.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 / Matric, or a relevant tertiary qualification
  • Aged roughly 18–30
  • Strong numeracy and communication skills
  • Unemployed and not studying full-time
  • Clear criminal and credit record

Stipend & What You Get

Standard Bank offers one of the higher banking-learnership stipends, around R7 500 a month, plus an accredited NQF 5 qualification, mentorship, and access to its graduate pipeline.
