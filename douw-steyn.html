Source of Wealth

Douw Steyn's fortune of around $1.5 billion in 2026 is built on the BGL Group, the UK insurance business behind comparethemarket.com and other direct-insurance brands, plus major property developments.

He pioneered direct and comparison insurance models that proved hugely profitable, and reinvested in landmark property including the Saxon hotel and the vast Steyn City development north of Johannesburg.

As a privately held fortune spanning insurance and property, the figure is an estimate.
