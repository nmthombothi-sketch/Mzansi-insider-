Source of Wealth

Gary Player's net worth of around $250 million in 2026 makes him one of golf's wealthiest figures, built far more on business than on prize money from his era.

A career Grand Slam winner with nine majors, 'The Black Knight' leveraged his fame into Gary Player Design, which has built hundreds of golf courses worldwide, plus apparel, branding, and bloodstock interests.

He is a model of converting sporting fame into enduring enterprise across decades. Figures are estimates.
