About This Bursary

The Nedbank External Bursary supports students in commerce, finance, accounting, and information technology — fields central to modern banking. It covers the full cost of study and provides mentorship and vacation work within Nedbank, offering a strong route into the financial-services and technology sectors.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Strong matric or current university results
  • Studying or intending to study commerce, finance, accounting or IT
  • Strong Mathematics results
  • Demonstrated financial need in many cases

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration fees
  • Accommodation
  • Books and a laptop
  • Mentorship and vacation work at Nedbank
  • Potential employment after graduating

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Nedbank bursary cover?
It typically covers full tuition, accommodation, books, and a laptop, plus mentorship and vacation work within Nedbank.
Which fields does the Nedbank bursary fund?
Commerce, finance, accounting, and information technology — fields central to modern banking.
Who can apply for the Nedbank bursary?
South African citizens with strong academic results, especially in Mathematics, studying or intending to study a relevant commerce or IT degree.
When does the Nedbank bursary close?
Applications generally close mid-year. Confirm the exact date on the official Nedbank careers site.
