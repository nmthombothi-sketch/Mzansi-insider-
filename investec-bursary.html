About This Bursary

The Investec Bursary supports talented students studying commerce, finance, accounting, and related fields. Beyond full financial cover, it offers structured mentorship and vacation work within Investec, giving recipients exposure to specialist banking and asset management and a strong route into a financial-services career.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Excellent matric or current university results
  • Studying or intending to study commerce, finance, accounting or a related degree
  • Strong Mathematics results
  • Demonstrated leadership potential and, in some cases, financial need

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration fees
  • Accommodation / residence
  • Prescribed books and a laptop
  • Mentorship from Investec professionals
  • Vacation work and potential employment

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Investec bursary cover?
The Investec Bursary typically covers full tuition, accommodation, books, and a laptop, plus mentorship and vacation work within Investec.
Who qualifies for the Investec bursary?
South African citizens with excellent academic results, strong Mathematics, who are studying or intending to study commerce, finance, accounting, or a related degree.
Does the Investec bursary include work experience?
Yes. It usually includes vacation work and mentorship at Investec, and can lead to graduate employment opportunities.
When does the Investec bursary close?
Applications for 2026 typically close mid-year. Always confirm the exact date on the official Investec careers site, as timelines change yearly.
