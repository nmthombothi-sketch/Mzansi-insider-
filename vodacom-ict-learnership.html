About This Learnership

Vodacom's SETA-accredited ICT learnership trains learners in information and communications technology, spanning network support, IT systems administration, and customer-facing technical roles. With ICT among the most in-demand skill areas in South Africa, this programme offers a direct route into a fast-growing, well-paying field, and Grade 12 with Mathematics is required given the technical content.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 / Matric with Mathematics
  • Aged 18–28 in most intakes
  • Interest in IT, networks and problem-solving
  • No similar learnership already completed

Stipend & What You Get

Learners receive a monthly stipend of around R5 800 over the 18-month programme while gaining accredited ICT training and hands-on technical experience. As with all learnerships, the stipend supports your living costs during training rather than acting as a market-rate salary.

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • IT support technician and network support roles
  • Systems administration positions
  • A foundation for further IT certifications (e.g. networking, cloud)
  • Entry toward higher-paying tech careers such as software and data
