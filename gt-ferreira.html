Source of Wealth

GT Ferreira's fortune, estimated at roughly $700 million, was built through the financial empire he co-founded — Rand Consolidated Investments, then Rand Merchant Bank (RMB), and ultimately FirstRand.

Alongside Laurie Dippenaar and Paul Harris, Ferreira built a banking group that became one of the largest on the continent, with his wealth held largely in FirstRand and related shares.

He has served as chairman of RMB Holdings and remains a respected elder statesman of South African finance.
