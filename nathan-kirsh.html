Source of Wealth

Nathan Kirsh's fortune climbed toward $20 billion in 2026, vaulting him to Africa's second-richest person behind Aliko Dangote. The trigger was Sysco's agreement in March 2026 to acquire his Jetro Restaurant Depot business in a deal valued at about $29.1 billion including debt — one of the largest food-distribution transactions ever, adding roughly $10 billion to his fortune.

His best-known asset, Jetro Restaurant Depot, became a multibillion-dollar wholesaler by supplying independent restaurants and small businesses that larger distributors overlooked — a quiet, low-margin model executed at vast scale.

Remarkably, he survived an early collapse that wiped out much of his wealth and rebuilt from a single cash-and-carry store, assembling a global portfolio across food distribution, property, and finance in near-total obscurity.
