About This Bursary

The MTN Bursary supports students in IT, engineering, computer science, and related commerce fields. As a leading African telecoms group, MTN invests in digital and engineering talent, offering full financial cover and the prospect of vacation work and employment.

Requirements

  • South African citizen
  • Studying or intending to study IT, computer science, engineering or related commerce
  • Minimum 65% average
  • Strong Mathematics results
  • Demonstrated financial need in many cases

What It Covers

  • Full tuition and registration
  • Accommodation and meals
  • Books, a laptop and allowances
  • Vacation work and mentorship
  • Possible employment at MTN

What does the MTN bursary fund?
