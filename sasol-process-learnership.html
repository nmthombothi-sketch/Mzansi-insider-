About This Learnership

Sasol offers some of the best-paid learnerships in the country, particularly in chemical plant operations, process, and instrumentation. Learners gain structured training plus real exposure to large-scale industrial operations at Secunda and Sasolburg.

Requirements

Who can apply
  • South African citizen
  • Grade 12 with Mathematics and Physical Science
  • Interest in chemical or process operations
  • Unemployed youth (roughly 18–35)
  • Willingness to work shifts at industrial plants

Stipend & What You Get

Sasol's learnerships are among the best-paid in South Africa, with stipends often around R7 000 a month, plus an accredited qualification in chemical plant operations and exposure to world-scale industrial facilities.

Application Process

Step by step
1
Apply via the Sasol careers and learnerships portal
2
Select a plant operations or process stream
3
Upload matric (with Maths and Science), certified ID and CV
4
Pass the assessments and psychometric testing
5
Complete checks and verifications, then begin if selected

Career Opportunities After Completion

  • Process / Plant Operator
  • Production Controller
  • Instrumentation Assistant
  • Process Technician (with experience)
  • A foundation for further chemical-engineering study
