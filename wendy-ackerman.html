Source of Wealth

Wendy Ackerman's fortune, estimated at around $300 million, comes from the Pick n Pay retail empire she co-founded with her late husband Raymond Ackerman in 1967.

She has served as a non-executive director of the group, and the Ackerman family owns a substantial stake in the JSE-listed company.

Widely regarded as one of South Africa's most influential businesswomen, she has devoted much of her later life to philanthropy.
