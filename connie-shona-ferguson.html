Source of Wealth

The Fergusons' combined net worth of around $12 million reflects their dual success as actors and as owners of Ferguson Films, the production company behind hit South African dramas like The Queen and Rockville.

Their wealth came from moving beyond performing into ownership — producing and selling shows to broadcasters, which captures far more value than acting alone.

Shona Ferguson died in 2021, but the production business and brand continue under Connie's leadership. As private-company owners, their figures are estimates.
