Source of Wealth

Ivan Glasenberg's roughly $11.2 billion fortune comes almost entirely from his shareholding in Glencore, the commodities trading and mining giant he led as CEO from 2002 to 2021 and where he holds the largest individual stake.

His wealth is tied to global trade in metals, oil, coal, and agricultural products. Glencore's 2011 IPO crystallised enormous paper wealth for its trader-owners, Glasenberg foremost among them.

Even after stepping down as CEO, his long-term shareholding keeps him among the wealthiest South African-born individuals, though Forbes excludes him from its SA list as he is resident abroad.
