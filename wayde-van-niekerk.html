Source of Wealth

Wayde van Niekerk's fortune of over $10 million in 2026 comes from prize money and a portfolio of endorsements built on one of athletics' iconic moments — his world-record 400m run.

At the 2016 Rio Olympics he won 400m gold and broke a 17-year-old world record with a time of 43.03 seconds, run remarkably from lane eight, instantly making him a global marketing asset.

He is also one of very few sprinters to post elite times across the 100m, 200m, and 400m, broadening his appeal to sponsors despite injury setbacks in subsequent years.
